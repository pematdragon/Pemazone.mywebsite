Welcome to My World

Who Am I?

My name is Pema Tshering. I'm someone who loves pushing my limits, exploring new things, and improving myself every day. Whether it's training in Muay Thai, getting better at boxing, playing games competitively, or learning about new places— I always aim to grow, learn, and become stronger physically and mentally.

My Passion for Muay Thai & Boxing

Martial arts is not just a sport for me—it’s a lifestyle. It teaches discipline, mental strength, and self-control. Every punch, every kick, every round helps me become tougher and more confident.

My Gaming Life

Gaming is more than entertainment—it’s a different world. I love strategy games, shooting games, and competitive challenges. They test your thinking, reactions, teamwork, and focus.

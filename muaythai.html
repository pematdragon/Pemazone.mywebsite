Muay Thai & Boxing

The Art of Eight Limbs & The Sweet Science

What is Muay Thai?

Muay Thai is a traditional martial art from Thailand known as the “Art of Eight Limbs” because it uses punches, kicks, elbows, and knees. It focuses on power, timing, and conditioning. Fighters train intensely to build strength, flexibility, and mental toughness.

What is Boxing?

Boxing is a combat sport focused on punches, footwork, and defense. It is known as the “Sweet Science” because of its strong emphasis on timing, precision, speed, and strategy.

Differences Between Muay Thai & Boxing

  • Muay Thai: punches, kicks, elbows, knees, clinching
  • Boxing: punches only, heavy focus on footwork
  • Muay Thai: more full-body striking
  • Boxing: better for hand speed & movement
